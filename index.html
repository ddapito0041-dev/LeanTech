About Scoliosis:


Possible Treatments for Scoliosis

Scoliosis treatment varies with severity and growth. Mild cases are often managed through observation with regular check-ups and X-rays, while bracing helps control progression during growth if worn consistently. Physical therapy strengthens muscles, improves posture, and keeps the body flexible, often alongside other treatments. For severe curves that affect daily life, surgery such as spinal fusion may be recommended to stabilize the spine and improve comfort.

Helpful Lifestyle Practices

Living with scoliosis doesn’t mean stopping your life, it means choosing smart habits like gentle exercises such as swimming, yoga, and walking, keeping good posture with ergonomic support, stretching daily to stay flexible, maintaining a healthy weight, and avoiding heavy lifting or high‑impact moves so your spine stays strong and you feel more comfortable every day.
